Quality Training

'Our tag line Quality Training is our motto'; this is what makes us the best in the industry. For many reputed companies, Felix-ITs is the number one choice for recruiting mobile app developers.

We are associated with a dedicated Industry Collaboration division and have a wide network across the country. Hence, our students are not limited to the opportunities available in Pune, but in all major cities in India.

Felix ITs' Student Job Portal

Felix have it's own job portal where student will get daily job notifications. Student just have to show there interest on the same app.

If students are interested Felix IT's HR will call you to confirm your interest but before that you have complete following things -

Complete your course.

Submit your Project/Portfolio.

Get review from your faculty.

Have practice mock interview session with HR.

Submit your updated CV.

Felix ITs' HR Portal

Felix HR portal maintains all information about the placement drive. HR will inform all the students who are eligible for placement drives. This portal maintains all the progress about students' performance and gives feedback as per students' performance.

HR will give feedback about your job interview if you are not get selected, and what was the reason why the company did not hire you. So, you can improve yourself and give your best performance in the next interview.

Have Mock Interview With HR

Our HR will take your mock interview to improve your communication skills. You will get instant feedback from HR and will help you how you can improve your skills, how to tackle the questions in interview.
